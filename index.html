Human Activity Detection

The Human Activity Detection project utilizes Python and machine learning models like ResNet to build an intelligent system capable of recognizing and classifying various human activities. By analyzing image or video data, the system can identify activities such as walking, running, sitting, and standing. This project finds practical applications in video surveillance, health monitoring, fitness tracking, and sports analysis, providing valuable insights into human behavior and movement patterns.

Store App

The Store App is a mobile application developed using Dart language and Flutter framework. It offers three main pages: Login, Shop, and Cart. The app provides a smooth and visually appealing user experience with engaging animations. Users can log in, browse through a variety of products in the shop, and add items to their cart. The Cart page allows users to review their selected items and proceed to checkout. Overall, the Store App offers a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience on mobile devices.
